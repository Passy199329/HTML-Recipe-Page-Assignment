Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian pasta dish made with rich meat sauce and traditional flavors.

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Fresh basil and grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer uncovered for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain pasta and serve topped with Bolognese sauce.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan if desired.

Cooking Tips

For richer flavor, let the sauce simmer longer or make it a day ahead. You can also add a splash of red wine while cooking the beef for added depth.
